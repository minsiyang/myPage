About Me

I am a full-stack junior developer, recently graduated from the Makers Academy coding bootcamp in London. I am looking to join a team where I can consolidate and expand my programming knowledge.

Being a creative individual, and having seen how the music industry could benefit from having a closer and broader relationship with modern technology, I decided to take the plunge to join the 16-week Makers Academy bootcamp after spending 20 years playing music with many orchestras and ensembles around the world.

I consider myself an energetic and outgoing person, with a real passion for learning new technology and an interest in problem solving. Collaborating with and meeting people from diverse cultures, age groups and backgrounds was one of the key elements of my previous work experience, which I would love to bring to my new work environment. I am eager to work in a supportive and diverse environment.
